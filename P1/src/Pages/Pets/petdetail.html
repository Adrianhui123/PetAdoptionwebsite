    Trixie is a vibrant 4-year-old Golden Retriever mix. She's been in excellent health throughout her life. Trixie was vaccinated recently, and her vaccination card is up-to-date. She underwent a minor surgery at the age of two to remove a harmless cyst from her back, and she's recovered perfectly. She also takes a monthly heartworm preventive pill.

    Trixie is a bundle of joy, always ready to play and be by your side. She's incredibly friendly and gets along with other dogs and even cats. Having been a family pet, she's great around kids and has a gentle demeanor. Trixie does have a quirk - she's scared of thunderstorms and tends to seek comfort during one. She's also trained well and responds to basic commands like "sit", "stay", and "come". She loves her toys, especially her squeaky rubber duck.

    Diet-wise, Trixie is not very picky. She thrives on a balanced diet, with a mix of dry and wet dog food. However, she does have a mild allergy to chicken, so it's best to avoid foods containing chicken or chicken by-products. Trixie loves the outdoors and requires at least two walks a day. She has a slight limp on her left hind leg due to an old injury, so she shouldn't be over-exerted with very long walks or strenuous exercises.
